04 — currently building

Opportiq

◎ Solo Founder · Product Research & Planning

"Turn time into experience, and experience into opportunity."

Opportiq is a product idea I'm developing — a collaborative platform for unemployed developers, fresh graduates, and career switchers who have skills but no verifiable, real-world experience to show for it.

The platform would let users join structured, team-based projects, contribute to real tasks, and build a proof-of-work profile — a verifiable record of what they actually shipped and how they collaborated, beyond a traditional resume or portfolio.

I've completed a full Business Requirements Document covering user flows, functional requirements, core features, KPIs, and a phased roadmap for future AI integration — skill matching, resume auto-generation, and a recruiter dashboard. Currently in the research and architecture planning phase before implementation.
